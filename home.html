Welcome To U.S. Pizza

U.S. Pizza is all about quality you can trust. As one of the original founding Pizza brands and the 3rd largest Pizza chain in India, our sole mission is making the freshest, tastiest and cheesiest Pizza around. We offer a variety of delicious pizzas, including:

  • - Veg Pizza: A flavorful combination of fresh vegetables like bell peppers, onions, tomatoes, and mushrooms on a bed of mozzarella cheese.

  • - Cheese Pizza: A classic favorite loaded with melted mozzarella cheese, perfect for cheese lovers.

  • - Paneer Pizza: A popular choice featuring paneer (Indian cheese) marinated in spices and herbs, adding a rich flavor to the pizza.

  • - Classic Pizza: Our classic pan pizza will always be a fan favourite, with a soft and chewy crust perfectly balancing out the healthy tomato puree and mozzarella - cheddar blended cheese.

  • - Pepperoni Pizza: A spicy and savory option featuring pepperoni slices, perfect for meat lovers.

  • - Pan Pizza: A thick-crusted pizza cooked in a pan, offering a crispy crust and a hearty portion.

Our pizzas are made with love and care, using only the freshest ingredients. You can choose from our authentic Italian crust or our classic pan crust, and pair it with one of our delicious puree sauces - spicy buffalo, sweet BBQ, tangy chipotle, or creamy makhni. Our suggestions of combinations might be helpful, but ultimately the power lies with you to create your perfect pizza.
